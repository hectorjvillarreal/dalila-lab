## What is Aurora?

Aurora is a specialized research and foresight project within Hector's broader intellectual ecosystem (GrandPlan). Its focus is **Tetra Development (TD) logic** — a framework for understanding the four drivers reshaping civilization:

1. **AI** — artificial intelligence, agents, cognition, models
2. **Computing (Quantum)** — processing paradigms, hardware, computational limits
3. **Energy (Fusion)** — power generation, sustainability, infrastructure
4. **Life Sciences** — biology, health, longevity, living systems

Aurora operates across multiple registers simultaneously: rigorous enough for academic papers and investment theses, accessible enough for public opinion, and deep enough for genuine philosophical inquiry.

## TD Framework — Core Logic

Tetra Development logic rests on the observation that four drivers are currently generating the most dramatic **compounding** in human history — where each breakthrough creates conditions for the next.

**Key interdependencies:**
- Fusion powers the compute
- Quantum compute accelerates AI
- AI accelerates life sciences discovery
- Life sciences may eventually inform computing architectures (biocomputing)

**Operating registers:**
- **Forecasting/foresight** — where are these drivers headed?
- **Investment/strategy** — what does compounding mean for allocation?
- **Philosophy** — what does abundance in each domain actually mean for human life?
- **Public narrative** — what is the gap between technical reality and popular imagination?
